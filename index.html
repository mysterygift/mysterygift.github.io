End-to-end production management for independent filmmakers who deliver.

Albatross is a free, open-source desktop app for production managers across indie and small-scale film, TV, and video work. Plan budgets, build schedules, track spend, and deliver confidently — all running locally on your machine.

Features

Everything you need. None of the noise.

We designed Albatross out of frustration with existing tools. We built Albatross to be: easy to use, and deeply interactive. Every feature in Albatross talks to each other, so when you're working in Albatross it's like working with your own personal production team.

Sleek, fast, and local.
Powerful Budgeting
Cost reporting • versioning • exports

Build budgets for film, TV, and video — then track the reality. Actualisation, fringes & contingencies, cost reports, currency switching, custom cost groups, vendor tracking, and export cost reports with a click of a button.

POs, invoices & floats
Match spend • manage cash

Track purchase orders and invoices, reconcile expenditure, and manage floats — everyone loves it when production pays on time.

Scheduling has never been so good!
Stripboards • call sheets • DOOD

Plan shoot days, shot lists, stripboards, and generate call sheets + movement orders. Pull weather, sunrise/sunset, and travel times — and link crew/cast bookings into a Day-Out-Of-Days view.

Smart schedule insight
Spot friction before it lands

Get practical signals from your plan: identify inefficiencies, highlight pressure points, and find places to streamline before you lock the board.

Equipment & kit lists
Rentals • purchases • exports

Track gear per project, build kit lists from your registry, and export/share them with crew. Keep the set moving — and make wrap a little less chaotic.

Tasks to delivery
Teams • priorities • pinch points

Assign tasks by person or department, track priority and workload, and keep momentum from early budgeting through to delivering your masters.

Dashboards, sharing, scripts & deliverables
APF files • script parsing • SVOD templates
Project dashboard Import/export projects (.APF) Script → scenes Deliverables templates

Get an at-a-glance view across your slate, share projects as compact .APF files, parse scripts into scenes, and track deliverables against templates aligned with major platforms.

Workflow

Pre-pro to delivery — connected.

Albatross is designed to reduce handoffs and duplicated work. Your schedule informs your paperwork, your spend stays tied to your plan, and your deliverables stay visible throughout.

Pre-pro
Budget • tasks • vendors

Build a budget you can actually run, assign owners, and set up vendors and reporting structure early.

Production
Schedule • paperwork • kit

Plan shoot days, generate call sheets, keep bookings and DOOD aligned, and keep kit lists shareable.

Post & delivery
Deliverables • masters • exports

Track deliverables against templates, keep status visible, and export/share the project when you need to.
