Schedule

Plan faster. Shoot smoother.

Albatross connects shoot calendars, stripboards, and shot-list data into one scheduling system. Production managers get a real-time view of how planning choices impact call sheets, logistics, and day-to-day execution.

Operational upside

  • Coordinate units, scenes, and people from a shared planning source.
  • Reduce last-minute schedule churn with clearer conflict visibility.
  • Generate call documentation from current planning data.

Shoot-day calendars

Organize shoot days by phase, episode, or bloc and keep schedule structure clear as plans evolve.

Multi-unit stripboards

Use drag-and-drop stripboards across units with notes, timing context, and scene-linked planning.

Scene-linked shot lists

Tie shot planning directly to script-derived scene records to keep prep and execution aligned.

Day-level intelligence

Track call/wrap context, turnaround, script 8ths, and day shape to support realistic scheduling decisions.

Episodic aware

Plan episodic productions with episode assignment and shooting bloc structure without losing clarity.

Smart schedule insight

Surface friction points and improve board flow before they impact crew, budget, or location logistics.

From planning to paperwork, connected.

When schedule data is the source of truth, call sheets and movement planning stay in sync with actual production conditions.
